Campus Life

Classroom

Founded in 1551

All of the KEGS classrooms can accommodate 28 students. A larger classroom area, or two standard classrooms opened up into one large space, which can be arranged in conference style with seating for up to 56. Computer suites are equipped with 30 network-connected computers.



Theatre

Founded in 2019

The McCallum-Rich Theatre was opened in September 2019 in one of the last stages of the improvements linked to the Nigel Fanshawe Sports Hall. The relocation of PE enabled the previous gym to be converted to a theatre, named in honour of John B. ‘Bil’ McCallum, Head of English and teacher at KEGS from 1966 to 1998 and also in honour of his wife Diana, née Rich, who enabled his career.



Sports Hall

Founded in 2018

This state-of-the-art facility opened in September 2018 and includes a large sports hall and a studio. Boasting five badminton courts, the sports hall is also laid out for volleyball, tennis, cricket, netball and five-a-side football. It is overlooked by a viewing balcony, allowing spectators to see all of the sporting action. The adjacent studio has a sprung wooden floor and is an excellent venue for gymnastics, aerobics, yoga, pilates, martial arts or any dance based exercise. Hirers would need to supply their own sound system. There are also male and female changing facilities.
